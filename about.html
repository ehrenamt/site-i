About Me

I am an upcoming university graduate, majoring in computer science and minoring in geography. I enjoy working with all types of technologies; you can just about catch my interest with anything involving a bit of hardware and software. My areas of focus include backend development, systems development, data analysis, and networking. I am also highly interested in the fields of aviation, GIS, transportation technology, and communications software.

I write this blog as a means of showcasing my critical thinking skills, sharing my interests, and letting people know what I have been up to lately. If you like any of these articles or would like to contact me, kindly send me a message using the link(s) at the bottom of the page. I enjoy receiving feedback.

I hope to use my technology skills for the greater good, and to encourage a love of learning and rigorous processes. I hope to be part of a positive change that changes technology to be safer, more usable, and more understandable for everyone. I wish for my software to be accessible to all, not exclusive to those with the most performant, most modern hardware.

If you are more interested in my professional experience and technical qualifications, kindly reach out to me for my resume.
