About Me

I am a software developer and GIS analyst with a degree in computer science with a minor in geography. I enjoy working with all types of technologies. My areas of focus include backend development,systems development, data analysis, networking, and security. I am also highly interested in the fields of aviation, GIS, transportation technology, and communications hardware and software. I currently work at an airport.

I hope to use my technology skills for the greater good, and to encourage a love of learning and and rigorous processes. I hope to be part of a positive change that changes technology to be safer, more usable, and more understandable for everyone. I wish for my software to be accessible to all, not exclusive to those with the most performant, most modern hardware.

I greatly enjoy learning foreign languages. It is simply a joy to learn new words and apply them to a new grammar, and to find oneself understanding interntional news in local languages. As such I am using this website to hone my skills in writing in foreign languages. I have much to improve, but I am certain it will be worth it. It is one of my personal goals to become a polyglot.
