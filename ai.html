The Usage of Artificial Intelligence

I do not utilize generative AI in any work available on this website.

I do not utilize generative AI in any posts in any language published on this site. All translated works are written by myself with the aid of both offline and online dictionaries in addition to occasional use of context dictionaries and conjugation tables.

Any works present on this site are mine alone. This work is meant to represent my efforts independently. The use of generative AI would be unethical and purposeless as I intend, through this website, to demonstrate to you, the reader, my capacity to 1) discuss, reason, and elaborate on software both holistically and in detail and 2) communicate clearly, effectively, and precisely in multiple languages.

Utilisation d’IA

Je n’utilise pas l’IA sur ce site web.

Je n’utilise pas l’IA à acune article en aucune langue publiee sur mon site web. Tous mes articles de blog sont ecrits, traduits, et relus par moi-même avec l’aide des dictionnares (en ligne et en format livre). J’utilise parfois des tableaux de conjugaison.
