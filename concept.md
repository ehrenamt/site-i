# Personal Site Concept

This website will serve two purposes equally.

The first purpose is to talk about technology, cities, and geography, such that employers are impressed by my skills in analysis, my skills in communication, and my level of detail and technical depth.

The second purpose is to focus on less technical and more experiential perspectives on computing and geography. We will take a social sciences approach rather than the natural sciences. This is again to demonstrate my critical thinking skills, but more importantly to A) clearly distinguish myself from other developers and B) to demonstrate my essay writing, social sciences, non-technical interests and skills, aimed at sharing my opinion and work to those who are not interested in employing me, but more interested in what I think and what I have been up to. TO let friends know what I write about, what I think about on the daily.

We understand this is not ideal and we will try to separate these two functions in the future.
